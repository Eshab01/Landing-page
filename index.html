Meet Scrappy Uncle

At EcoLoop, we're on a mission to solve grassroots waste issues through technology, empowerment, and sustainable practices. With over 62 million tonnes of waste generated annually, it's time for a smarter solution.

Our Mission

We connect waste generators with collectors and recyclers through a seamless digital platform that makes waste management efficient, rewarding, and transparent.

The Impact

Every pickup reduces landfill waste, empowers informal workers, and contributes to a cleaner environment. Track your impact in real-time through our dashboard.

Problems We Solve

Unstructured Waste

Residential and commercial waste is often unorganized, making collection inefficient and recycling difficult.

Recycling Bottleneck

Current systems lack the technology to effectively sort and process recyclable materials at scale.

Poverty & Informal Workers

Waste collectors often work in difficult conditions with minimal income and no formal recognition.
